## 📋 Before using the Mental Load app, please read the following terms carefully:

---

### 1. Consent to Data Collection and Use

✓ The data you enter (text or voice) will be used to analyze your cognitive load level and provide personalized recommendations to improve your productivity and mental wellbeing.

---

### 2. Data Storage and Service Improvement

✓ Your responses and usage history may be stored to improve the accuracy of future analyses and predictions. All data is encrypted (AES-256) and stored on secure servers (Supabase).

---

### 3. Microphone Access

✓ When using voice input, the app will request permission to access your microphone for the purpose of recording your voice and converting it to text using AI technologies (Whisper API).

✓ **Voice recordings will NOT be stored after processing** – they are deleted immediately after conversion to text.

---

### 4. Use of Data

✓ Your data is used solely to generate reports and personal insights within the app. Your data will not be used for any external purpose without your explicit consent.

---

### 5. No Data Sharing

✓ **Your personal data will NEVER be shared with any third party under any circumstances**, unless required by law (e.g., a court order).

---

### 6. Your Data Rights (Right to be Forgotten)

✓ You can:
- Modify your data at any time
- Permanently delete your account and all your data via Settings → "Delete Account"
- Export all your data in JSON format via Settings → "Export Data"

---

### 7. Medical Disclaimer (Important Notice)

> ⚠️ **This app provides supportive analyses and recommendations for self-awareness only and is NOT a substitute for professional medical or psychological advice.**
>
> **If you are experiencing severe symptoms (depression, anxiety, harmful thoughts), please stop using the app immediately and contact a mental health professional.**

---

### 8. Parental Consent (Under 18 Years)

✓ If you are under 18 years old, you must obtain parental consent before using the app.

**Procedure:**
1. Enter your parent's/guardian's email address
2. A verification link will be sent to your parent/guardian
3. After they approve, your account will be activated
4. Your parent/guardian will receive weekly aggregated reports about your usage patterns

---

### 9. Policy Updates

✓ This policy may be updated from time to time. You will be notified of any material changes via:
- Your registered email address
- An in-app notification
